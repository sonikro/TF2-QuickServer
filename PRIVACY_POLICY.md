# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: December 14, 2025**

## Introduction

TF2-QuickServer ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Discord bot service.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our service.

## Information We Collect

### Limited Data Collection

TF2-QuickServer does not track or collect any personal information from users beyond what is necessary to provide our service.

### Data We Store

The only information we store is:

- **Discord User ID** and associated **SteamID**

This association is collected solely to grant Sourcemod permissions and provide administrative access to Team Fortress 2 servers created through our bot.

## How We Use Your Information

We use the stored Discord User ID and SteamID association exclusively for:

- Granting appropriate Sourcemod permissions on Team Fortress 2 servers
- Authenticating users for server administration
- Maintaining server access and permissions

## Data Security

We implement appropriate technical safeguards to protect the limited information we store against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

## Data Retention

We retain your Discord User ID and SteamID association only for as long as you actively use our service. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

## Third Parties

We do not share, sell, or disclose your information to third parties except as necessary to provide the core functionality of our service (such as communicating with Steam and Discord APIs).

## User Rights

You have the right to:

- Request access to your stored data
- Request deletion of your data
- Opt-out of our service at any time

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any significant changes by posting the updated policy and updating the "Last Updated" date above.

## Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us through our Discord support channels.
